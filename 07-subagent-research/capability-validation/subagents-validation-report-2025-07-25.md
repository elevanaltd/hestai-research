## Risk Assessment

### Identified Risks
1. **Context Window Limitations**: Subagents may have constraints on large codebases
2. **Multi-Role Coordination**: Single-agent focus vs orchestration needs
3. **State Management**: Limited persistence between invocations

### Mitigation Strategies
1. Chunk large analyses into focused tasks
2. Use zen-mcp for multi-role coordination
3. Maintain context through explicit state passing
