## Evolution Timeline

### Phase 1: Thymos Era (Orchestral)
- **Challenge**: Can multi-agent AI systems coordinate effectively?
- **Solution**: Orchestral metaphor with specialized roles
- **Result**: Proved coordination possible but revealed complexity challenges
- **Evolution Pressure**: Need for simpler, more intuitive approaches

### Phase 2: Daedalus Era (Workshop)  
- **Challenge**: How to maintain power while reducing complexity?
- **Solution**: Workshop metaphor with three simple commands
- **Result**: Breakthrough in usability and adoption potential
- **Evolution Pressure**: Identity crisis - roles needed capability flexibility

### Phase 3: HestAI Era (Constitutional)
- **Challenge**: How to maintain role identity while allowing capability flexibility?
- **Solution**: SHANK-ARM-FLUKE architecture separating identity from capabilities
- **Result**: Constitutional resolution enabling stable, flexible system
- **Current State**: Mature architecture ready for implementation
