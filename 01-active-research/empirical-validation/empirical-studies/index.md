## Summary of Key Empirical Findings

1. **Architecture Patterns Work**: Two-phase architecture, modular design, and constitutional approaches demonstrably prevent common failure modes
2. **Implementation Gaps Matter**: Many systems show excellent design (90%) but poor implementation (40%)
3. **Constraints Create Value**: Sequential processing, boundary enforcement, and role separation produce measurably better outputs
4. **Cross-Model Validity**: Frameworks like RAPH and OCTAVE work across different LLM architectures without modification
5. **Human Orchestration Essential**: Systems work best with human oversight at key decision points
6. **Simple Beats Complex**: Operational triggers and simple principles outperform complex protocols
7. **Mathematical Principles Hold**: Divine proportion and other mathematical relationships consistently emerge in effective systems

These studies provide concrete evidence that the theoretical frameworks have been tested, validated, and refined through practical implementation and empirical measurement.
